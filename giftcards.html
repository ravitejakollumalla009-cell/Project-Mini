Not only a birthday, but you can opt for a gift card for a plenty of other occasions, tailor-made to suit your requirements. The procedure to make a gift card purchase is extremely convenient and hassle-free. Click on the Buy gift card option. Select the occasion and personalise the message, if you want. Enter the lucky person’s e-mail address, enter the value of the gift card that you want to purchase and the recipient’s name. Post preview, proceed with the payment using your debit or credit card or with the help of internet banking. Once the payment is confirmed, Bingo! Your gift card is on the way! The best part about Mynn gift cards is that it has been designed to suit most of the special occasions. Additionally, the process of redeeming is as simple as it is for purchasing. Add the gift card to your Myntra account by clicking ADD TO ACCOUNT in the email sent to the recipient containing the Gift Card details or directly add the gift card from Manage Gift Cards. Once the gift card is added successfully in your Mynn account, the gift card balance will appear on the payment page during checkout. Select Gift Card at the time of payment during checkout.
