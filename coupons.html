Types of Coupons on Mynn
  • Percentage Discounts – e.g., Flat 20% OFF on orders above ₹1999.
  • Flat Discounts – e.g., ₹500 OFF on ₹2499 purchase.
  • Bank & Wallet Offers – Extra discounts on payments made with specific banks (HDFC, ICICI, SBI, etc.) or wallets (PhonePe, Paytm).
  • First-time User Coupons – Special discount for new customers installing the app.
  • Seasonal & Festival Coupons – During End of Reason Sale (EORS), Diwali, Big Fashion Festival, etc.
  • Category Coupons – Specific to shoes, ethnic wear, beauty products, or accessories.
